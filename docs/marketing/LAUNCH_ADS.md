## Brand Direction

Healthcare-blue, calm, credible, operationally complete. This should feel like production-grade SaaS for home healthcare operators, not a generic AI demo.

Type and color cues:

- Display font: Inter Tight or Sohne, semibold for headlines.
- Primary blue: `#0B5FB1`
- Accent teal: `#10A4A4`
- Surface white: `#F7FAFC`
- Status success: `#1F9D55`
- Status attention: `#D97706`
- Logo lockup: white logomark plus `RayHealthEVV™` wordmark on blue end frames; navy mark on white.

Compliance copy rule:

- Never imply RayHealthEVV directly files taxes or executes wage payments.
- Use "payroll readiness", "approvals", "exports", or "authorized payroll-provider handoff".

Voiceover pacing target:

- 150 wpm, roughly 75 words per 30-second spot.
- Where copy is shorter, use the breathing room for music swells or held end frames.

## Production Checklist Before Shipping

- Domain: use `rayhealthevv.com` everywhere.
- Trademark: confirm `RayHealthEVV™` versus a registered mark before final render.
- Talent and b-roll: prefer real caregivers and clients with signed consent and release.
- Captions: burn captions into all paid social cuts for Meta, TikTok, and LinkedIn.
- Legal/compliance review:
  - Confirm "EVV verified" and "21st Cures Act ready" match current product behavior.
  - Keep payroll language to readiness, approvals, exports, or authorized payroll-provider handoff.
  - Confirm GPS and PHI claims match actual app behavior and privacy disclosures.
- A/B variants:
  - "Care, on the same page." vs. "Run the agency, not the spreadsheet."
  - "Book an agency demo" vs. "Start a free trial", depending on go-to-market state.
- Tracking:
  - UTM-tag every end-frame URL by ad and placement.
  - Suggested audience pages: `/agency-demo`, `/caregivers`, `/family`, `/compliance`.
